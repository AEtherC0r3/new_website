An open space to meet, learn and share.

A 120m2 space dedicated to creative software and hardware hacking. Tech communities, meetups and individuals they now have their own space in Thessaloniki.

Learn

Stay up to date with interactive weekly presentations and workshops of cutting edge technologies.

Make

A 3D printer, electronics and a fully equipped lab will help you prototype your idea.

Connect

Makers, coders, ninjas, engineers and designers meet here. Get in touch with the city's brightest minds.
